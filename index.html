Image Processing

Digital image processing techniques applied to lightning analysis using computer vision algorithms (MATLAB)

üé• Slow Motion Image Processing Algorithm

Algorithm Overview

A slow-motion algorithm is applied to reduce playback speed and analyze the formation and propagation of the lightning channel in more detail.

  • Raspberry Pi camera V2 capture at 60 FPS
  • Temporal interpolation algorithms
  • Motion vector analysis
  • Frame-by-frame lightning tracking

‚ö° Observations from Slow-Mo Lightning:

  1. Step leader starts jumping towards the ground, creating a path for the lightning channel.
  2. Upward leader from the ground meets the step leader and connects.
  3. Return stroke happens immediately after the connection ‚Äî this is the brightest flash.
  4. Dart leader follows, sending additional negative charge from cloud to ground (causes flicker).
  5. Dart leader lightning has the longest duration time.

üßπ Image Restoration

Noise can be introduced due to electronic devices interference (Raspberry Pi Camera V2), environmental conditions such as rain, dust, fog, and night-time darkness. To mitigate these effects, high-pass and low-pass filtering techniques are applied:

  • Median filter to enhances edges and sharpens important details in the image.
  • Averaging filter to smooths the image.
  • Motion blur correction to correct blurriness.
